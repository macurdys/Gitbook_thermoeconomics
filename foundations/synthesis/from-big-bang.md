# From Big Bang to Blockchain

> **Essay + Conversations:** Steph Macurdy, January 2026
>
> Two conversations tracing the full arc from cosmological entropy production through biological computation to blockchain consensus.

## Overview

How does the universe's thermodynamic arrow connect to the emergence of digital economic systems? These dialogues trace the thread from the Big Bang's low-entropy initial state through the emergence of structure, life, intelligence, and finally proof-of-work consensus as the latest expression of nature's tendency to produce complex, self-maintaining systems at entropy gradients.
